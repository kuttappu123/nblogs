What is a Pentest Framework?

A pentest framework is a critical approach or way to structure a penetration test using a variety of tools, which can best serve the pentesters during the engagement. Although there are many frameworks that can be used, the two most common frameworks for penetration testing are the Metasploit Framework and the Cobalt Strike Framework. The Cobalt Strike framework is a collection of other tools that work independently of the Metasploit Framework.
